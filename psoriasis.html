Understanding Psoriasis

Psoriasis is a chronic autoimmune skin condition that causes rapid skin cell growth, resulting in thick, scaly patches. It affects millions worldwide and can significantly impact quality of life. Our holistic approach treats the condition from within, addressing immune dysfunction and triggers.

Types & Symptoms

  • Red, raised patches with silvery scales
  • Dry, cracked skin that may bleed
  • Itching and burning sensations
  • Thickened or ridged nails
  • Scalp psoriasis
  • Joint pain (psoriatic arthritis)
