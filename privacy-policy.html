Privacy Policy

Your privacy and confidentiality are our top priorities

Home > Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Homoeo Amigo, operated by Bhargava Phytolab Pvt Ltd, is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our healthcare services.

Your Trust Matters: We understand that your health information is highly sensitive and personal. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy protection and compliance with applicable healthcare privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, age, gender, and contact information (phone, email, address)
  • Medical history, symptoms, and health conditions
  • Treatment preferences and consultation records
  • Payment and billing information
  • Emergency contact details

Technical Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Website usage data and cookies
  • Device information and access logs
  • Chat and communication records

Health Information

  • Current symptoms and medical complaints
  • Previous medical treatments and medications
  • Family medical history (when relevant)
  • Consultation notes and treatment plans
  • Progress reports and follow-up information

3. How We Use Your Information

Medical Care and Treatment

  • Providing personalized homeopathic consultations and treatment
  • Maintaining accurate medical records
  • Monitoring treatment progress and outcomes
  • Coordinating care between healthcare providers

Communication

  • Scheduling appointments and follow-ups
  • Sending treatment reminders and health tips
  • Responding to inquiries and providing support
  • Emergency contact in case of urgent health matters

Service Improvement

  • Improving our healthcare services and website functionality
  • Analyzing treatment outcomes (in anonymized form)
  • Training healthcare professionals
  • Research and development of new treatment protocols

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Limited Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal health information. We only share information when necessary for your care or as required by law.

We may share your information with:

  • Healthcare Providers: Other healthcare professionals involved in your care
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist with our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court orders, or regulatory authorities
  • Emergency Situations: To protect your vital interests in medical emergencies

We will NOT share your information for:

  • Marketing purposes without your explicit consent
  • Commercial gain or profit
  • Non-medical research without your permission
  • Social media or public platforms

5. Data Security

Security Measures

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with restricted access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Staff training on privacy and security protocols
  • Password protection and multi-factor authentication

Physical Security

  • Locked filing cabinets for physical records
  • Restricted access to medical records areas
  • Secure disposal of sensitive documents
  • CCTV monitoring of premises

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your medical records
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Obtain your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Request limitations on how we use your data
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your information

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Website Data

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

We provide healthcare services to patients of all ages, including children. When treating minors (under 18 years), we:

  • Obtain consent from parents or legal guardians
  • Maintain the same privacy protections as for adult patients
  • Follow applicable laws regarding minors' healthcare privacy
  • Respect age-appropriate privacy rights

9. International Data Transfers

Your personal information is primarily stored and processed in India. If we need to transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your privacy rights.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal health information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide ongoing healthcare services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Maintain medical records as required by law
  • Handle any legal claims or disputes

Medical records are typically retained for a minimum of 7 years after your last consultation, or longer if required by law.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Notify you of any material changes
  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Update the "Last Modified" date
  • Obtain your consent for significant changes when required
