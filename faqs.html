Medicines

Homeopathy is a natural system of medicine founded over 200 years ago by Dr. Samuel Hahnemann. It is based on the principle of "like cures like" - using highly diluted substances to stimulate the body's natural healing response.

Homeopathic medicines are prepared from natural sources like plants, minerals, and animal products, making them safe and free from side effects.

Yes, homeopathy is completely safe for people of all ages, including infants, children, pregnant and breastfeeding mothers, and elderly patients.

Since homeopathic medicines are highly diluted and made from natural substances, they don't cause any harmful side effects or interfere with other medications.

The response time varies depending on the condition and individual. For acute conditions, improvement can be seen within hours to days. For chronic conditions, it may take weeks to months for significant improvement.

Factors like duration of illness, severity, age, and general health condition influence the healing time.

Our treatment process begins with a detailed consultation where we understand your complete medical history, symptoms, lifestyle, and emotional state.

Based on this comprehensive evaluation, we select the most suitable homeopathic remedy that matches your unique symptom picture. Regular follow-ups help us monitor progress and adjust treatment as needed.

Yes, homeopathic medicines can be safely taken alongside conventional medications as they don't cause any drug interactions.

However, it's important to inform your homeopathic doctor about all medications you're currently taking for better treatment planning.

Homeopathy can effectively treat a wide range of conditions including allergies, asthma, skin disorders, digestive problems, hormonal imbalances, joint pain, mental health issues, and chronic diseases.

It's particularly effective for conditions that tend to recur frequently or those that haven't responded well to conventional treatment.

The first consultation typically lasts 60-90 minutes. This detailed session helps us understand your complete health picture including physical symptoms, emotional state, lifestyle, and medical history.

Follow-up consultations are usually shorter, lasting 30-45 minutes.

Yes, we provide online consultations for your convenience. You can book video consultations through our website and receive the same quality of care from the comfort of your home.

Medicines can be delivered to your doorstep following the consultation.

Please bring all your medical reports, previous prescriptions, and a list of current medications. Also, prepare a detailed description of your symptoms and how they affect your daily life.

The more information you provide, the better we can understand your case and provide effective treatment.

Homeopathic medicines are usually taken on an empty stomach, at least 30 minutes before or after meals. Avoid strong flavors like mint, coffee, or camphor around the time of taking medicine.

Follow the specific dosage and timing instructions provided by your doctor for best results.

Generally, there are no strict dietary restrictions. However, it's advisable to avoid excessive coffee, alcohol, and very spicy foods as they may interfere with the medicine's action.

Your doctor will provide specific dietary guidelines if needed based on your condition.

If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember. However, if it's almost time for the next dose, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular schedule.

Don't double the dose to make up for the missed one. Contact your doctor if you have concerns about missed doses.
