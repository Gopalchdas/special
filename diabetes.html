Understanding Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition that occurs when your body cannot effectively use or produce insulin, leading to elevated blood sugar levels. There are two main types: Type 1 (insulin-dependent) and Type 2 (non-insulin dependent). Proper management is crucial to prevent complications.

Key Symptoms

  • Increased thirst and frequent urination
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Extreme fatigue and weakness
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow healing wounds
  • Tingling in hands and feet
